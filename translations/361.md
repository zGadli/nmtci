# Chapter 361: Zhang Yu's Weakness Period

Inside the dormitory, Le Mulan produced a bottle of black potion and said, "I brought the potion for you."

She hesitated, then asked, "But are you sure you want to use it?"

Zhang Yu took the potion, gazing at the faint cloud-like patterns shimmering within the liquid. "Don't worry," he assured her. "I can handle it."

Since becoming Director Gao's Registered Disciple, Zhang Yu had gained access to a wider range of medicinal pills and now enjoyed discounted access to many experimental drugs from the Pharmacy Institute's laboratories.

However, while these experimental drugs boasted remarkable efficacy, they often came with severe side effects. Over the past few weeks, Zhang Yu had been working with Le Mulan to carefully screen them.

After numerous trials, he had finally settled on the Demonic Mimicry Aerosol before him.

Staring at the bottle, Zhang Yu thought, "My mental cultivation technique has already reached the Spirit-Flesh Unity Cultivation Realm, operating constantly to temper my dao heart."

"My Magic Power also circulates constantly thanks to the Passive Breathing Technique, requiring only occasional Spirit Stone consumption."

"Only my physical strength... The majority of my current cultivation efforts are focused on advancing the Martial Dao Sacred Body cultivation arts. While these techniques enhance combat and construction skills, they aren't specifically designed for body refinement."

"Although this process does gradually improve my physical strength, it's far too slow compared to dedicated body refinement techniques."

Zhang Yu felt the fiery energy coursing through his body and thought it would be a waste not to properly refine his body after consuming the Yang-Enhancing Meat.

"Then, wouldn't I hold it in for nothing?"

He twisted open the medicine bottle, opened his mouth, and inhaled. A cloud of mist surged from the bottle, writhing like a living creature as it burrowed into his body.

Immediately, Zhang Yu began practicing Spring and Autumn Endless Zen for body refinement.

As he finished his body refinement session, he felt the Demonic Mimicry Aerosol transform into countless tiny hands, pummeling and twisting throughout his body. They continuously wore down his flesh, but this process also tempered and refined it.

This refinement suddenly ignited the residual fire from the Yang-Enhancing Meat within him, nourishing his flesh and blood.

"This is the Demonic Mimicry Aerosol's effect — it simulates the effects of a Body Refinement Technique, maintaining some of the benefits even after you stop training."

"But the downside is... once you start, it inevitably lasts for several days, potentially causing severe damage to your physical body, or even death."

Still, Zhang Yu was confident he could endure it thanks to his resilient constitution.

What truly prevented him from using the aerosol long-term was its exorbitant price.

"Even after the discount, a single bottle costs nearly 0.9 Spirit Coins," he grumbled. "Fucking expensive."

Zhang Yu was already quite familiar with Le Mulan's classmate. After all, Xuan Ge had closed the door for them countless times, taken out their trash repeatedly, and even expressed his desire to share a room with them by submitting multiple dormitory applications.

At that moment, Zhang Yu's gaze fell on Xuan Ge's chest. "Blazing Sun Meridian? Does this guy also have a Dao Seed?" he wondered.

Sensing it, Zhang Yu realized the Blazing Sun Meridian was a Magic Power-type Dao Seed, just like Le Mulan's, requiring an increase in Magic Power Quality to activate.

"150% Magic Power Quality, huh?"

Meanwhile, Xuan Ge grew increasingly nervous under Zhang Yu's intense scrutiny.

Suddenly, Zhang Yu spoke up. "Xuan Ge, right? Let me give you some pointers."

Instructing Le Mulan to leave first, Zhang Yu led Xuan Ge into the dormitory.

Xuan Ge excitedly followed him inside, his mind racing, "Is it my turn? Is it finally my turn?"

He asked, "Should I wash up first?"

Zhang Yu frowned. "What's there to wash up for?"

Just as Xuan Ge was wondering why this didn't match what the tutorial had said, Zhang Yu had already pressed a palm against his back.

In the next moment, two opposing streams of Magic Power — one icy cold, the other scorching hot — collided within Xuan Ge's body.

Xuan Ge immediately sensed a visible improvement in his Magic Power Quality. The fiery energy within him seemed stimulated, circulating at an accelerated pace and dramatically enhancing the efficiency of his breathing technique far beyond its usual limits.

Simultaneously, Zhang Yu's Bionic Eye flickered, signaling an incoming communication from Bai Zhenzhen.

Bai Zhenzhen: "Yu Zi? What are you up to?"

Zhang Yu swiftly rotated the camera, capturing his surroundings. "Preparing to sell Magic Power," he replied matter-of-factly.

He then deliberately focused the camera on Xuan Ge for an extended moment. "This is my trading partner," he explained. "Feeling relieved now?"

Bai Zhenzhen scrutinized Xuan Ge. After confirming the other party was male, she nodded in relief.

She knew Zhang Yu had always lacked talent in the Dual Cultivation Dao, and his compatibility range for partners was extremely narrow. Only biological females could trigger the necessary internal reactions within him.

Bai Zhenzhen sighed inwardly. "If you had any aptitude for dual cultivation, I'd be the first to support you as your best bro. But without any talent, if it's purely for personal interest... isn't this just wasting your cultivation time?"

Zhang Yu retorted, "A-Zhen, I told you not to worry. With my iron willpower, I can hold back, right?"

Bai Zhenzhen countered, "It's not that Mom doesn't trust you. I'm just afraid you'll get distracted by frivolous pursuits and neglect your studies."

"Then keep up the good work! I'll send you more meat in a few days."

After hanging up, Zhang Yu continued channeling Magic Power into Xuan Ge for another ten minutes.

Withdrawing his hand, Zhang Yu looked at Xuan Ge and asked, "So, what do you think? Want to buy some Magic Power?"

Xuan Ge thought, "Ah, so he's here to sell me Magic Power?"

Though slightly disappointed, Xuan Ge recalled the sensation of absorbing the Magic Power earlier and thought, "It feels like the same cold attribute Magic Power as Le Mulan's."

"It's highly beneficial for improving my Magic Power Quality and enhancing my breathing technique efficiency."

"And judging by his tone, he wants to bypass the Platform and conduct a private transaction. I should be able to get a better price that way."

After some negotiation, they agreed on a private deal: Xuan Ge would purchase 10,000 units of Magic Power from Zhang Yu monthly at a rate of 1 Spirit Coin per unit.

Watching Xuan Ge's retreating figure, Zhang Yu mused, "I wonder what the effects of this Blazing Sun Meridian are."

Regardless of its effects, the guaranteed monthly income was a welcome addition to his finances.
