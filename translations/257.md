# Chapter 257: Defeating Wang Yin

"I told you, I've got you."

Looking at the astonished Wang Yin before him, Zhang Yu let out a furious roar, already grabbing and shifting his opponent's center of gravity, then viciously slamming him onto the ground.

Wang Yin, who was busy suppressing the magic power in his body, remained motionless, only able to watch Zhang Yu's actions helplessly.

Boom!

As the ground quaked, Wang Yin's body instantly sank, smashing a large crater into the earth.

In the midst of the intense concussion, Wang Yin's brain slammed back and forth inside his skull, leaving him feeling dizzy.

---

Zhang Yu's mind recalled True Person Starfire's guidance: "That bionic skin is impervious to blades and swords, and resistant to fire and water. Even if you trained for another year, you probably wouldn't be able to break it."

"However, in combat, you must learn to avoid strength and strike at weakness."

"Don't immediately think about breaking the skin. Instead, first bypass the skin and look for an opportunity to use concussive force to rattle Wang Yin's brain."

"Then, vibrate his entire body to sever the connection between his flesh and the skin... do you understand? That skin of his is a bionic, not real skin."

"It's like a power socket, plugged into his flesh."

"As long as you vibrate it repeatedly, and violently, you can cause a bad connection."

---

Amidst a series of thunderous booms!

Wang Yin had become like a great hammer in Zhang Yu's hands, being smashed into the ground again and again and again.

During the violent tremors, his brain, floating in cerebrospinal fluid, was continuously shaken as he was flipped up and down and slammed repeatedly, causing drastic changes in his intracranial pressure.

At the same time, the once-tight connection between the bionic skin and his flesh and blood began to loosen under this vibration.

Boom!

As Wang Yin's head was once again viciously slammed into the earth, sending out shockwaves, a bruise finally appeared on his forehead.

Zhang Yu, who had been observing this scene intently, narrowed his eyes, knowing his chance had come.

Although it currently looked like Zhang Yu was overwhelming Wang Yin, he knew his severely injured body couldn't hold out for long.

Conversely, while Wang Yin appeared battered, he hadn't sustained much damage and was only temporarily under control. The spiritual root in his body was constantly healing his injuries, and the turbulent magic power was gradually calming.

With one waning and the other waxing, if Zhang Yu couldn't find a winning opportunity, defeat was only a matter of time.

But now, the opportunity was here...

Zhang Yu thought, _The appearance of the bruise indicates a problem with the connection between the skin and the flesh there. It's a flaw in the bionic skin created by the vibrations._

_According to True Person Starfire, it's like the socket has come loose..._

As the thought crossed Zhang Yu's mind, the Soul-Wounding Brick that True Person Starfire had placed in his sea of consciousness trembled slightly.

At the same time, Wang Yin felt the world spin around him. His bones rattled as if they were about to fall apart, and his head and ears buzzed as if a million mosquitoes were vibrating their wings inside his skull.

Finally, he felt everything come to a stop. He was now lying quietly on the ground.

Then Wang Yin slightly raised his head and saw a golden light flash in Zhang Yu's hand as it came smashing down toward his forehead.

With a loud thud!

As the Soul-Wounding Brick and Wang Yin's head collided fiercely, a resonant sound, like the tolling of a great bell at dusk, emanated from the point of impact.

Zhang Yu saw what appeared to be countless flashes of light and shadow on the Soul-Wounding Brick, which then poured through the flaw in the bionic skin at the forehead and into Wang Yin's mind.

"Spirit Realm Golden City, deposit 10,000 and get 10,000 free! Casino direct!"

"Super electronic entertainment, exciting, hot, and realistic!"

"Gold-medal local tutoring, raise your score by hundreds in a month, register for free and get a forbidden drug."

"Heavenly Court Sunshine Project, Private Mutual Aid Finance! Get rich together!"

In just an instant, Wang Yin's brain was flooded with a massive amount of junk information.

Wave after wave of spam crashed against his mind like a tsunami, leaving him in a daze.

And watching the densely packed, countless things that flashed for a moment and poured into Wang Yin's mind like a raging tide, Zhang Yu was slightly taken aback.

_Is this... advertising?_

_No, these are all scam ads, aren't they?_

_And they seem very much like the scam ads in the style of evil gods._

Zhang Yu looked at the Soul-Wounding Brick and thought, _So it wasn't a brick._

_This thing is actually a hard drive, isn't it?_

Many thoughts raced through Zhang Yu's mind, but it was all in the span of an instant.

Just as he raised the Soul-Wounding Brick to strike a second time.

Wang Yin let out a furious roar and threw a punch at Zhang Yu.

At this close-quarters combat range, Zhang Yu had nowhere to dodge.

And having already been grievously wounded, he knew he couldn't take the hit head-on.

_Then let's risk it all._

So Zhang Yu chose to sacrifice an arm and continue to smash the brick down on Wang Yin.

Boom!

Zhang Yu's left arm moved to block Wang Yin's full-force counterattack.

Even though he did his best to absorb the force with his apertures, as the power traveled through his arm, it still shattered his bones and burst his muscles, sending a spray of blood into the air as his entire arm dropped limply.

And as the Soul-Wounding Brick thudded against his forehead once more, Wang Yin's whole body convulsed.

He clenched his fists, wanting to continue his counterattack, but to his horror... he found it difficult to even focus his attention.

Watching the Soul-Wounding Brick about to fall again, Wang Yin glared at Zhang Yu and snarled, "Zhang Yu!"

"Don't you want your money anymore?"

"Have you forgotten the hundreds of millions frozen in your and Bai Zhenzhen's accounts? And the tens of millions that still haven't been paid to you."

"As long as you're willing to continue admitting your mistake, you can keep going. I'll double the money..."

Zhang Yu cut him off directly, his voice cold. "The frozen money... should have been our payment for the job in the first place!"

"If you won't give it, I'll take it back myself!"

Thud! With a loud bang, Wang Yin cried out in pain, feeling as if his brain was about to explode from the sheer volume of information being crammed into it.

He threatened furiously, "Zhang Yu! You dare to harm my soul! The Oasis Group! Netherworld University! The Netherworld Palace will not let you go!"

Zhang Yu raised the Soul-Wounding Brick high again, looking down at Wang Yin from above, and said coldly, "Damn you... you never let me go in the first place!"

Thud! With another loud bang, Wang Yin bled from all seven orifices. As his brain overloaded, his eyes could no longer see clearly.

Wang Yin struggled to speak, "The fate of the first layer... was decided... by the Immortals... you are defying the grand scheme..."

"The grand scheme?" Zhang Yu said faintly. "If I follow the grand scheme, I get eaten!"

Thud!

"If I endure, I still get eaten!"

Thud!

"Since Kunxu is either eat or be eaten, then I'll eat you first!"

Thud!

By now, Wang Yin's head was slightly deformed, his entire face a bloody mess. Although his limbs were still twitching slightly, he made no other movements and spoke no more words.

The very next moment, True Person Starfire appeared at Zhang Yu's side.

With a single sweep of his hand, he shattered the Netherworld Blood-Shadow Veil. The thousands of blood-shadows let out a long sigh of relief, finally dissipating into the air inch by inch.

Zhang Yu's raised arm was gently held by True Person Starfire. "That's enough, child."

"Any more, and he'll die..."

"Things like him are everywhere up there; his death is of no consequence."

"You don't need to be buried with him."

"In my eyes, you are far more precious than he is."

With heavy breaths, Zhang Yu gradually calmed down.

He looked up to see that the area outside the battlefield had erupted into a chaotic brawl, with countless Oasis Group employees trying to rush in to save Wang Yin.

But more people rose up, standing in the way of the Oasis Group.

The great battle between the two sides stretched from the sky to the ground, like some kind of chain reaction that was about to turn the entire Songyang City into a corporate battlefield.

Zhang Yu said, "Are these all freelance personnel? So many came?"

Zhang Yu thought to himself that Deng Bingding was quite capable, to have gathered so many people.

True Person Starfire, however, just smiled faintly and said, "Hehe, you could say that. But these people's other identities are the local magnates of Songyang City, employees of other corporations, and even some from the Oasis Group."

Zhang Yu was slightly taken aback. "They're Deng Bingding's freelance support? Then before..."

True Person Starfire said nonchalantly, "They help whoever is winning."

"With Wang Yin defeated, everyone will understand that this was arranged from above, the result of a power struggle up there."

"Now, the vast majority of magnates on the first layer, especially those who were previously suppressed, are fighting back against the Oasis Group. Other corporations and employees are just fishing in troubled waters."

Zhang Yu looked at the fiercely fighting experts and asked, "Aren't the righteous gods going to stop them?"

"Why should they stop it?" True Person Starfire said coolly. "A moderate amount of war can promote the real economy, boost the medical market, make insurance companies pay out more, and also reduce employment pressure and eliminate outdated technology."

"To the Netherworld Palace, the problems of the first layer need to be solved through reform, through breeding farms."

"But to some people, perhaps the problems of the first layer can be solved with just a few battles."

Hearing this, Zhang Yu was at a loss for words for a moment. He looked at Wang Yin on the ground and said, "What about him? What do we do with him now?"

"It's not for us to handle." True Person Starfire led him away. "Someone will naturally come to take him back."

"When you go to university in nine months, you'll likely have to face him again."

"At that time, there won't be so many people who can help you."

True Person Starfire looked at Zhang Yu and said, "For the next nine months, you and Bai Zhenzhen will study with me."

---

On the street.

Li Xuelian invited the Immortal Resonance Group's director, "Together?"

The director shook his head. "I'm under contract, and it doesn't expire until tomorrow. You go ahead."

Li Xuelian smiled faintly, and the next moment her figure flickered, a trail of silver light following her long hair.

And wherever Li Xuelian passed, the employees of the Oasis Group fell to the ground, unharmed but unconscious.

---

In front of a phone screen.

Yu Xinghan watched the scene of True Person Starfire leading Zhang Yu away, and only one thought filled his mind.

It's over!

The sky collapsed for the Golden Core disciple!

Yu Xinghan clutched his chest, feeling a sharp pain.

"Zhang Yu will take at least half of my training funds..."

But then he remembered the image of Zhang Yu rushing into the Netherworld Blood-Shadow Veil and smashing Wang Yin half to death with a brick, one blow after another, and Yu Xinghan shook his head.

"No, he'll probably take more than 80% of the training funds."

"And Bai Zhenzhen, if you count her..."

"It's over. I probably won't even get one percent of the old man's inheritance."

"Making a little pocket money in the future will be as hard as ascending to heaven."

Suddenly, something occurred to Yu Xinghan. He immediately picked up his phone and quickly opened Zhang Yu's crowdfunding loan information.

"Zhang Yu won big this time, so he's definitely going to make a fortune."

"But as long as I continue to provide loans, to keep lending him money, then no matter how much he earns, I can get a 1.3 times return from him..."

But just as Yu Xinghan excitedly clicked in, he found that the loan channel had already been closed.
